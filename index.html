Philadelphia Food Access

An interactive map of how easily Philadelphians can reach a grocery store or farmers market on foot, by bike, or by car. Use it to spot neighborhoods with strong access — and the gaps in between.

1
Pick a travel mode (walking, cycling, driving) and a time threshold (5, 10, or 15 minutes) in the sidebar. The access surface uses a single warm color across all thresholds — only the area changes.
2
Click anywhere on the map to drop a pin and see what is reachable from that point — or click a store or market icon directly.
3
Five recommended grocery sites (gold squares) show where a new store would close the biggest access gaps. Click any card to fly to that spot.
